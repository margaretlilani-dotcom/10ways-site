How I Work

A clear process. No black boxes.

Every engagement is tailored, but the framework is consistent — diagnose where you are, design what's next, and deliver results.

Step 01

Diagnose

Deep-dive into your current GTM motion — what's working, what's not, and where the biggest leverage points are. I talk to your team, study the data, and map the landscape.

Step 02

Design

Build a focused, actionable GTM plan — not a 60-page deck that collects dust, but a clear roadmap with priorities, owners, and timelines your team can execute on.

Step 03

Deliver

Roll up my sleeves and work alongside your team to implement. I stay embedded through execution — hiring, launching, iterating — until the engine is running.

About

The person behind the practice.

Margaret Lilani

Founder, 10ways

I've spent 20 years building go-to-market teams and revenue engines across SaaS, regulated industries, and professional services. I've led global teams of 5,000+ people across 90+ countries — driving expansion in LATAM, EMEA, and APAC — and built revenue from zero to over $100M in ARR.

The name 10ways comes from something my father used to say: "There are 10 ways of saying something — use the good ones." That idea is the foundation of everything I do. In go-to-market, there are always multiple paths. My job is to help you find the right ones — the ones that actually work for your stage, your market, and your team.

I started 10ways because I kept seeing the same patterns: talented founders with strong products who were struggling to crack the GTM code. I help them skip the expensive trial-and-error and build the right foundation from the start.
